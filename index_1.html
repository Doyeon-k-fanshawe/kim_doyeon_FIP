About Us

Please feel free to open the door to the book garden. We are confident that we will always provide you with the best service.

As soon as you enter the book garden, a new world will unfold in front of your eyes. You can fall in love with a nice person, go to another world you've never been to, and become a friend with historical figures.

Did you happen to know? Every time you order a cup of coffee, tea, or dessert, you're helping our local farmers and giving some funding to support our agricultural communities! That is, you can enjoy our all-natural ingredients and be the sponsor of our farm!

Our services include:

- Book Searches: we can search any book from our database
- Book Orders: in-store, by phone or online.
- Significantly Discounted Books: more books added weekly
- Book Club Event: If you have participated in the event at least once, you will get a 10% discount.
- Coffee coupon: If you collect ten stamps, you get a discount of 3 dollars.


Book Garden's always waiting for you to come to meet us!
